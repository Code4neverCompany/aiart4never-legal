Privacy Policy — AIart4never

Last updated: 13 June 2026

AIart4never ("we", "us") is a creator project that produces original AI-generated artwork and short videos and publishes them to the creator's own social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains what data we handle and how.

1. Who we are

AIart4never is operated by an individual creator. For any privacy question, contact us at aiart4never@gmail.com.

2. What data we access

When you connect a social media account (e.g. TikTok) to AIart4never, we receive an authorization token that lets us publish content to your own account on your behalf. We use it solely to upload and publish the content you choose. We do not request access to your private messages, contacts, or browsing data.

3. How we use data

  • To publish the creator's own images and videos to the connected account(s).
  • To show the status of a publish request (success/failure).

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising.

4. TikTok

AIart4never uses the TikTok Content Posting API only to publish the creator's own content to the creator's own TikTok account. Our use of information received from TikTok complies with the TikTok Developer Terms and TikTok's policies.

5. Data retention & security

Authorization tokens are stored only as long as needed to provide the publishing service and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the account in your platform settings. We use industry-standard measures to protect any data we handle.

6. Your rights

You may revoke access at any time and request deletion of any data we hold by emailing us at the address above.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will reflect any change.
